The Problem Statement

Paradox of Choice

Traditional travel planning involves cross-referencing 10+ tabs, manually calculating travel times, and balancing budgets. This creates a "decision fatigue" that prevents users from enjoying the discovery phase.

  • [!] High cognitive friction in route optimization.
  • [!] Disconnected data points (Flights vs Local Spots).

The Math-Driven Solution

Algorithmic Harmony

We built a custom LLM-wrapper that treats itinerary planning as a Graph Theory problem. Locations are nodes; travel times are weights. The UI abstracts this complexity into a seamless natural language interface.

  • [✓] Vector-based personalization engine.
  • [✓] Real-time spatial re-calculation.
